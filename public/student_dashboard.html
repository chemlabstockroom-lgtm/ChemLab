Laboratory Policy Agreement

Please read and agree before accessing the system

By clicking I Agree, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and will comply with the following laboratory policies.

1

General Laboratory Conduct

  • ▸Food and drinks are strictly prohibited inside the laboratory.
  • ▸Students must wear appropriate Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) at all times.
  • ▸Chemicals must only be used as instructed by the assigned professor or lab technician.
  • ▸All spills, accidents, and breakages must be reported immediately.
2

Borrowing of Laboratory Materials

  • ▸Each group must designate a student monitor for every laboratory activity.
  • ▸Inspect borrowed items before signing the Borrower's Slip. Report existing defects immediately.
  • ▸All items must be returned clean and in good condition at least 30 minutes before the period ends.
3

Loss, Damage, and Deficiencies

  • ▸Any loss, breakage, or damage must be reported to the Laboratory Technician immediately.
  • ▸An incident report must be completed within 72 hours from the time of occurrence.
  • ▸Lab ID will be withheld until all obligations are properly settled.
4

Data Privacy and Information Usage

  • ▸Personal data is collected solely for lab identification and accountability purposes.
  • ▸Data is accessible only to authorized personnel — Lab Technician, Professors, and System Admin.
  • ▸Personal information is never sold or shared with third parties outside the institution.
